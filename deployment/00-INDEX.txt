  1 VM: VM.Standard.A1.Flex — 4 OCPUs, 24 GB RAM, 100 GB boot volume

This single VM comfortably runs all 12 PDFKit containers:
  api-gateway, pdf-service, conversion-service, storage-service,
  queue-service, organization-service, security-service,
  metadata-service, html-service, mysql, redis, minio

Estimated idle RAM usage: ~3-4 GB out of 24 GB
Estimated capacity: ~100-200 concurrent users
